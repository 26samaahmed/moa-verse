TOMORROW X TOGETHER

About TXT

TOMORROW X TOGETHER, read as Tomorrow by Together is a bright and youthful boyband aiming to become the next breakthrough artist.

The band is composed of five members: Soobin, Yeonjun, Beomgyu, Taehyun, and Hueningkai, where each member is different from one another, shining in their own way to create synergy.

Choi Soobin

Born December 5, 2000, Soobin is a South Korean singer who leads the boy group TXT. He is known for his strong vocals, charismatic stage presence, and leadership skills within the group. Soobin's role in guiding TXT's endeavors both on and off stage has been instrumental in their success since their debut in 2019.

Choi Yeonjun

Born September 13, 1999, is a South Korean singer and dancer. Renowned for his versatility in singing, dancing, and rapping, Yeonjun contributes significantly to TXT's distinctive sound and style. His dynamic performances and magnetic stage presence make him a standout member of the group.

Choi Beomgyu

Born on March 13, 2001, is a South Korean singer, dancer, and musician. Beomgyu is admired for his versatile talents, including his strong vocal abilities and dynamic dancing skills. His charming personality and stage presence have endeared him to fans, contributing to TXT's widespread acclaim and success in the K-pop industry.

Kang Taehyun

Born on February 5, 2002, is a South Korean singer, songwriter, and dancer. Known for his powerful vocals, smooth dance moves, and charismatic stage presence, Taehyun has played a significant role in TXT's rapid rise to stardom.

Huening Kai

Born on August 14, 2002, is a South Korean singer, songwriter, and musician. His outstanding vocal range, instrumental skills, and captivating stage presence make him a vital component of TXT's unique sound. With his charisma and talent, Kai has garnered global admiration and played a significant role in TXT's remarkable success in the music industry.
