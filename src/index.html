About TXT

TOMORROW X TOGETHER, read as Tomorrow by Together is a bright and youthful boyband aiming to become the next breakthrough artist.

The band is composed of five members: Soobin, Yeonjun, Beomgyu, Taehyun, and Hueningkai, where each member is different from one another, shining in their own way to create synergy.

Choi Soobin

born December 5, 2000, is a South Korean singer leading the boy group TXT. Renowned for his vocal prowess, stage charisma, and leadership, Soobin plays a vital role in guiding TXT's endeavors on and off stage
